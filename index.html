How It Works

Mint Your NFT

First, you mint a free NFT (Beta users only), and once you connect it the site, it starts generating a limited number of tokens.

Fuel W3Bot

The W3Bot uses the tokens as fuel and burns them as it executes tasks, like sending applications and downloading resumes.

Control W3Bot

The more tasks the W3Bot executes, the more tokens you burn. If you turn off the bot, then it stops burning tokens.

Re-energize

If you run out of tokens, you eith mint another one or buy from the secondary market. You'll be able to see how many tokens are in secondary market NFTs.
